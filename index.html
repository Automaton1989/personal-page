Why I'm changing careers?

Six Years ago, I tried to start a company in the Esports space. After four years of work, and a few pivots along the way, I was forced to close it down. My one regret is not knowing more about programming and development, after having to work with developers for the entire lifecycle of the company. So I decided to join a bootcamp, and then take part in the Northeastern Align program. As of right now, I have no regrets, and happy to be a Husky!
